Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website project takes 4-8 weeks, while more complex applications can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's your design process like?

We follow a collaborative three-phase approach: Research & Strategy, Design & Prototype, and Development & Launch. Throughout each phase, we maintain close communication and incorporate your feedback to ensure the final product exceeds expectations.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Absolutely! We offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website or application running smoothly. This includes security updates, content changes, performance optimization, and technical support.

What's included in your pricing?

Our project pricing includes strategy consultation, custom design, development, testing, deployment, and initial training. We provide detailed proposals with transparent pricing and no hidden fees. Additional services like ongoing maintenance are optional.
